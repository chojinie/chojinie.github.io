Hyunjin Cho is a researcher at the Manufacturing AI Research Center (MAIK), Korea Institute of Industrial Technology (KITECH), working with Prof. Jongpil Yun. He will join the University of Birmingham as a PhD student in Computer Science in Autumn 2026, supervised by Prof. Hyung Jin Chang and Prof. Aleš Leonardis, with research support from Google. Previously, He was a research intern at SNU AILab, and a Research Assistant at VILAB, Chung-Ang University, advised by Prof. Jongwon Choi. He also worked at Fasoo, an IT security company. He received an M.S. in Advanced Imaging Science from Chung-Ang University in 2025, advised by Prof. Jongwon Choi, and a B.S. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering, also from Chung-Ang University in 2019.
